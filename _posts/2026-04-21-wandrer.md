A few years ago I got into running, I could write a whole post about that but I wanted to chat a bit about the fun tool I discovered while getting into it. Like most runners I use [Strava](https://www.strava.com/athletes/97851041) to record my runs, but through a friend mentioning it I discovered [Wandrer](https://wandrer.earth/). Basically, you allow Wandrer to sync to Strava/Garmin/etc to track all the roads you've ever been to.

I've been on a quest to finish my home city (9.63% as of this time writing), and it's really forced me to get creative with my long runs. I'm planning on eventually biking the rest as some routes are highway or areas that wouldn't really be pleasant to run along.

90% of my runs still go along the same route unfortunately, but I'm slowly mixing it up.
